About Me

I'm an English teacher in Toronto, originally from Montreal. I have been teaching English for more than 20 years. I help many people, like new Canadians, foreign workers, and students from around the world.

English is my first language, but I also speak French and Spanish. I understand how hard it can be to learn a new language. I have a degree in Linguistics and Translation, so I know a lot about how to learn and teach languages. My lessons are made for each student, so you can learn in the way that is best for you.

I look forward to helping you reach your English goals!
